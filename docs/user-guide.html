Andalanku User Guide

Learn how to use Andalanku features to optimize your business operations

Dashboard

The Andalanku dashboard presents a visual summary of all important aspects of your business in one view.

Tips: You can customize dashboard widgets according to your needs by clicking the settings icon in the top right corner of each widget.

Main Dashboard Features:

  • Financial Summary widget displays monthly income and expenses

  • Sales Trend Graph with comparison to previous periods

  • Inventory Status with minimum stock alerts

  • Employee Activity and current shift schedule

Employee Management

Easily manage employee data, attendance, and performance using the Employee Management module.

How to Add a New Employee:

  • Click the "Add Employee" button in the top right corner

  • Fill in the employee data form completely

  • Upload photo and supporting documents

  • Set access rights and department

  • Click "Save" to complete

Inventory System

Monitor and manage stock items with an integrated inventory system.

Important: The system will automatically notify you when stock reaches the predetermined minimum limit.

Inventory Features:

  • Real-time stock in and out recording

  • Barcode system for product tracking

  • Item categorization and grouping

  • Automatic stock movement reports

Financial System

Manage your business finances with an integrated and easy-to-use system.

Financial Modules:

  • Income and expense transaction recording

  • Invoice and payment management

  • Automatic tax calculation

  • Comprehensive financial reports

Reports

Access various types of reports for better business analysis.

Report Types:

  • Financial Reports (Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet)

  • Inventory Reports

  • Employee Performance Reports

  • Business Trend Analysis

Tips: You can export all reports in PDF or Excel format for further analysis.
